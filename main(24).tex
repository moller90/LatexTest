We assume $ f_{u \nu} $ denotes a maximum flow in $ G_{u \nu} $.
For any $ u \in V $, the edge connectivity $ k $ equals $ \min\limits_{ \nu \in V-\{u\}}  |f_{u \nu }| $.\\
